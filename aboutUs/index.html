The Story..
Everything was not the same when I joined my family business. It was just 150 sq.ft store, with just 3 employees and roughly 500-800 customers and an average yearly turnover of 40 lakhs.
In one decade we grew from 40Lakh to 500 crore turnover. Of course thanks to my family members who purely focused on purity and establishing trust.
The right strategy alone was not enough for this success but the execution was.
But to execute this, the cost we paid was
  • Not even a single holiday or a small break
  • Working 14+ hours every day
  • A hurry hustle lunch times and some times not even that
  • Immense mental stress
  • No family time, not attending even close relations wedding and the list goes on.
But during this journey, a lot of times, for a lot of problems, I used to think what if I had a “so and so solution” to do my “so and so work” and “solve so and so problem”.
This was how this start up was born, With a vision to digitize the Gold Industry
Our Mission
To transform the way jewellers are doing the business from the current old fashioned way with lot of barriers to grow to a better simplified, structured growth.
What We Do?
At Jewellers.live, we are creating a complete university for the jewellers to Learn all the aspects needed to create growth strategy including marketing plan, training their employees, hiring and team forming, accountings and legal frameworks. Then execute it with our new generation modern tools in a simpler and easier way and grow not just with respect to not only the business alone but with respect to establish themselves among the community of like minded jewellers.
